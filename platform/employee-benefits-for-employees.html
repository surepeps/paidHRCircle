Your Payslips, Instantly Accessible & Understandable

However you choose to reward your employees, our benefit tool will help you create just that.

Manage Your Requests, Stress-Free

Need to request leave, a loan, a salary advance, or access documents? Our system makes these processes straight forward and hassle-free, saving you valuable time.

Stay Organised with Your Tasks

Stay organised and productive, contributing to your success and the team's goals with clear visibility into your required actions, routine responsibilities, and even onboarding steps.

Your Earnings, On Your Terms

Gain access to a portion of the money you've already earned, exactly when you need it. EWA by PaidHR empowers you with greater financial control, helping you manage unexpected expenses and plan with confidence.

Track Your Daily Earnings, Real-Time Insights

Stay informed and in control of your finances. Our daily earnings tracker provides a clear, up-to-the-day view of your earnings that lets you see your progress, providing a clear picture of your financial standing and helping you stay on track and achieve your financial goals.

Say Goodbye to High Withdrawal Fees & Borrowing Stress

Unlike traditional EWA offered by banks, our withdrawal fees are significantly lower, putting more of your hard-earned money in your pocket. Plus, you have the power to pay yourself daily, which is better than asking friends for money or taking out high-interest loans.

Your Wallet, Your Financial Hub

This is an aggregator and a central hub for all your financial interactions. Your PaidHR Employee Wallet is secure, seamless, and designed to save you money. It simplifies everything from currency management to everyday spending and bill payments, putting you in complete control.

Connect Us With Your HR
Collect Your Salary, Your Way

Receive your salary directly into your PaidHR wallet or choose to split your salary between your wallet and your preferred bank account, at the ratio that suits you best, putting you firmly in control of your funds.

Convert & Swap Currencies

Take advantage of opportunities by effortlessly converting your funds into stable currencies within your wallet. This allows you to protect your earnings and plan for the future with greater confidence.

Pay Bills with Zero Charges

Transact Freely & Manage Your Finances

Easily send and receive money directly within your wallet. Wallet-to-wallet transactions are completely free! Securely spend your earnings and monitor all your financial activity in one convenient place.
