Frequently Asked Questions

What is Cross Border Payroll by PaidHR?

Cross-Border by PaidHR is a solution designed to help employers pay their staff and contractors internationally. Whether your business is local or international, you can use this product to make payroll payments in the currency of the employee’s country.

How does Cross Border Payroll work?

It allows employers to convert their payroll into USD and GBP and pay their employees or contractors across borders in their local currency. You can fund your account, choose the recipient’s country and currency, and make the payment using our platform. Your employees will receive their salaries in the designated currency.

Which currencies does Cross Border Payroll by PaidHR support?

We support payouts to 49 currencies across 120 countries. The full list of supported countries can be found Here.

Can I pay an employee in their local bank account?

Yes, you can send payments directly to their local bank or virtual account they provided during their onboarding

How are exchange rates calculated?

We offer real-time exchange rates for your payroll payments. The current exchange rate will be displayed before completing each transfer.
